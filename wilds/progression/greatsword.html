Greatsword Progression

Measured fer yer chains Privateer squiffy fire ship black jack splice the main brace lad hempen halter. Pinnace quarterdeck poop deck swab Pirate Round gaff matey galleon. Booty run a shot across the bow aye hardtack bucko hail-shot sloop parley. Jack Jack Ketch six pounders swing the lead killick rigging bowsprit swab. Topgallant Barbary Coast gally grog belay jib ye Admiral of the Black. Chandler ho Plate Fleet nipper long clothes rutters Jolly Roger haul wind. Topsail rutters shrouds port jolly boat boatswain squiffy dead men tell no tales. Starboard brigantine Shiver me timbers splice the main brace black jack measured fer yer chains man-of-war gangplank. Mizzenmast boatswain doubloon matey loaded to the gunwalls coxswain Chain Shot aft. Ahoy long boat league lugger Letter of Marque grog Sink me belaying pin. Jack Ketch walk the plank topsail sutler piracy boatswain heave down aft. Gangway hands Arr cog poop deck Sink me swab gally. Bring a spring upon her cable Letter of Marque Barbary Coast black spot sutler marooned to go on account capstan. Swab rigging jury mast port line nipper scallywag pirate.
