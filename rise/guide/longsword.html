Basic moves:

  • Axe - Fade Slash: Backwards+X after any move that ends in axe
  • Axe - Overhead Slash, Forward Overhead Slash: Forward+X
  • Morph: ZR after any attack, sword->axe, axe->sword only if you have gauge
  • Dodgemorph: ZR after any dodge
  • Sword - Double Slash: A
  • Sword - Overhead Slash: X
  • Drawmorph: ZR while moving at all
  • Finishing/Zero Sum Discharge: X+A, then X repeatedly
  • Sheathe: Y
  • Dodge: B
Wirebug moves:
  • Soaring Wyvern Blade, Invincible Gambit, Wirestep*Sunbreak Exclusive: ZL+X
  • Switch Charger: ZL+A
  • Elemental Burst Counter*Sunbreak Exclusive: ZL+A->ZR
Important Combos:
  • Morph Loop: From axe: ZR->ZR+A->ZR
  • 2-Staged Morph Slash Combo*Sunbreak Exclusive: From axe: Wild Swing (A)->ZR->ZR(optional)
